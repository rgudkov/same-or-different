# Streak breaks strictly on any missed day — no grace period or freeze

Missing one local calendar day with no completed Daily resets Current streak to 0 immediately, with no forgiveness mechanism (no "one free miss per week," no streak-freeze token). This was a deliberate choice over the more forgiving models common in habit apps (Duolingo-style streak freezes), made specifically to keep the initial data model and logic simple (one date comparison, no freeze-balance bookkeeping) and to avoid coupling the streak feature to monetization before either exists independently. The obvious next step — "let a player watch an ad to restore a broken streak" — was explicitly considered and deferred, not rejected: it's a natural fit once web ads ship, but building it now would have made the streak feature's initial release depend on the ad integration's timeline. A future engineer reintroducing grace periods should treat it as an intentional product change, not a bug fix.
